About me!

Hello, I am a kid who lives in California.
My name is Peter and I am 12 years old.
I have one brother who is 8 years old.
I like many colors such as the ones that make up the rainbow.
Some of my hobbies are reading, making origami, doing sudoku, playing sports (such as tennis, swimming, and skiing), playing chess, and solving a Rubik's cube. I am trilingual, as I can speak English, Chinese and French.

Why I like Rubik's Cubes

I like Rubik's cubes because they present a challenge for people due to the millions of combinations they can have.
I also like solving one because they give my brain a challenge by having me recognize certain patterns shown on the cube and then having to execute a certain sequence of moves in order to solve it.
The cube not only trains my fingers but also my hand-eye coordination as I have to turn the cube as fast as possible in order to get a good solve time.

Why I like Black Holes


I like the black holes that exist in outer space because they are very interesting.
Black holes can suck in anything and everything, including light which I feel is quite impressive because theoretically, nothing can go faster than the speed of light.
Also, nothing can escape from a black hole, so it can hold the title as the universe's 'best escape-proof prison'.
It is also quite amazing how the black hole can hold all the mass it takes in in such a small space (which scientists call the singularity).
